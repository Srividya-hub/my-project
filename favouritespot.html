Top Picks for You

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an immense white marble mausoleum, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favorite wife. The Taj Mahal is the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of world heritage.

Paris

Paris is the capital and largest city of France. With the 17th-century legacy, Paris has been one of the world's major centers of finance, diplomacy, commerce, fashion, and culture. Known for its leading role in the arts and sciences, it is a city of life and a wonderful place to visit.

Himalayas

The Himalayas is a mountain range in Asia, separating the plains of the Indian subcontinent from the Tibetan Plateau. The range has some of Earth's highest peaks, including Mount Everest. More than 100 peaks exceeding elevations of 7,200 meters (23,600 feet) lie in the Himalayas.

Ooty

Ooty is a town and hill station in India state of Tamilnadu. The name"Ooty"is short for Ootacamund (offically udhagamandalam).Ooty is located in Niligir district, or blue Mountains a mountain districts. Ooty stands about 7,500feet above sea leavel,it is the queen of the hill station.
